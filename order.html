About Us

Welcome to My Gym, your number one source for all things. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of services, with a focus on dependability, customer service and uniqueness. Founded in 2022 by Adity Sharma, My Gym has come a long way from its beginnings in a gym equipent seller. When Aditya Sharma first started out, his passion for helping other to setup their gym, providing the best equipment for his fellow gym lover drove him to do intense research, quit his day job, and gave him the impetus to turn hard work and inspiration into to a booming online store. We now serve customers all over the India, the world and are thrilled to be a part of the fair trade wing of the sports industry. We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us. Stay fit and healthy.
